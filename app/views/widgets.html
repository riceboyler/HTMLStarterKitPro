Widgets

Widgets are pieces of reusable data-bound markup, featuring independent views/viewmodels. They are very similar to Angular directives, and are inspired by the future technology of Web Components.


KnockoutJS actually introduced the component binding in version 3.2 (which came out a few weeks ago), which in many ways supercedes widgets.
